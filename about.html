ABOUT

I’m a half-Swiss, half-Aussie (Australia, not Austria) artist currently based in Bern, Switzerland. I find most of my inspiration by the sea. I grew up having the carpet pulled out from under me regularly, my parents themselves creatives that moved us to a new country every two years or so. Whilst the lack of stability was challenging, it pushed me to explore, experiment, and find my creative voice from an early age onwards.

I’m inspired by risk-takers, pioneers and nature. I love it when people challenge norms. My personal style is high contrast - a combination of what I like, feel and see. There's always has a story hidden somewhere between the lines. Sometimes on them too.

My roots are in traditional paint, illustration, airbrushing, and writing. In recent years, I've focused on producing, directing, and editing in branded content, promo videos, short films, and documentaries.
